Our competitive advantage

Using reusable rockets to exploit the benefits of economies of scale, SpaceX cut costs to orbit by a factor of 18 and captured a large percentage of the global launch market, once thought to be inaccessible to newcomers in the industry.

Landing Legs and Grid Fins
The rockets are equipped with deployable landing legs and grid fins.
Merlin Engines
The rockets are powered by Merlin engines, which are designed for reusability.
Autonomous Flight Control
SpaceX rockets feature advanced autonomous flight control systems that manage the complex maneuvers required for launch, separation, and landing.
Heat Shield and Thermal Protection
The rockets have heat shields and thermal protection systems that safeguard critical components during re-entry.
Propellant Management
SpaceX has developed efficient propellant management systems that optimize fuel use during the various phases of flight.
Rapid Turnaround Capability
One of the key features of SpaceX's reusable rockets is their ability to be rapidly refurbished and relaunched.
